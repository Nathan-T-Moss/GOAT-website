My G.O.A.T. - King Gizzard and The Lizard Wizard

King Gizzard and The Lizard Wizard is an Australian experimental band combining sounds from all over the world, such as jazz, folk, metal, pop, and progressive rock.

Their discography consists of 45 studio and live albums, as well as 60 music videos. Their prolific nature and ever-changing musical style means that there is an album for everybody!

In a world where artists constrain themselves to a specific style of music, King Gizzard and The Lizard Wizard continue to push musical boundaries with each new release.
